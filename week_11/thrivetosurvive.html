Thrive To Survive

Mission: "Thrive to Survive is dedicated to providing support in a variety of ways to adults living in the Geneva, NY area diagnosed with cancer. Through this support, Thrive to Survive hopes to relieve unnecessary stress on the patient allowing them to focus on their recovery and their families. The ultimate goal of Thrive to Survive is to be able to support adults with cancer emotionally and financially while giving the hope to Thrive to Survive."

Where: Geneva, New York
